Do It

A direct accountability tracker. Gym, chores, the dog, and whatever your wife just asked you to do — all in one list you can't quietly ignore.

  1. Create an Airtable base with two tables: Templates and Log.
  2. Templates fields: Title (text), Category (single select), Days (multiple select: Mon/Tue/Wed/Thu/Fri/Sat/Sun), Time (text), Active (checkbox).
  3. Log fields: Title (text), Category (text), Type (text), TemplateId (text), Date (text), Status (text), CompletedAt (text), Notes (text), CalendarEventId (text).
  4. Create a Personal Access Token (airtable.com/create/tokens) scoped to that base with data.records:read and data.records:write.
  5. Paste the base ID and token below. This app creates and manages the Log rows for you — you don't need to touch them manually.
